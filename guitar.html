I created this little webapp, to help guitarist on learning their instruments, specifically where the harmonics are on a guitar. On every fret.

What I mean by this, is instead of scuffling around and trying to find that harmonic while holding down on the 9th fret; you can now see where it is visually on the guitar through this.

What is a harmonic?

A harmonic is a note that registers higher
